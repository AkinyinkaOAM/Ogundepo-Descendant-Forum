Adele Ogundepo

Adele Ogundepo stands as the founding patriarch of the Ogundepo lineage. His vision, discipline, and leadership laid the foundation upon which generations have flourished. Known for wisdom and resilience, Adele guided the family through defining moments in history.

His legacy continues through strong family values, unity, and respect for tradition.
